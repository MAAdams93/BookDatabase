When was Miyamoto Musashi alive?

Musashi's exact birth date is unknown, but is believed he was born in 1584, during the Azuchi-Momoyama Priod (The final days of the Sengoku Period). He died on the 13th of June, 1645, during the Edo Period. His cause of death is suspected to be lung cancer. He was survived by his adopted children and apprentices.
