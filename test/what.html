What is the Book of Five Rings?

The Book of Five Rings is a book of swordsmanship and philosophy, written by Miyamoto Musashi. It details his beliefs on life and how to become a better swordsman, and is split into five chapters:

  • Earth: Introduction and metaphorical discussion of martial arts and leadership.
  • Water: Basic tecniques and fundamental of Musashi's self made swordsmanship style, Ni-ten Ichi-ryu.
  • Fire: Discussions of the experience of battle and timing.
  • Wind: Exploration of the faults of various combat styles.
  • Void: Epilogue reflecting on Musashi's philosophy and influences, as well as his thoughts on proper mindset.
